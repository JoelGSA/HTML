Course Structure

The Inclusive Customer Service course provides a contemporary understanding of disability, accessibility, and inclusion, and addresses barriers for people with disability in everyday life. The course reinforces learnings through the use of supporting videos and quizzes.

Module 1
Understanding disability, accessibility, and inclusion
  • Understanding disability
  • Models of disability
  • Understanding accessibility
  • Inclusion in practice
Module 2
Inclusive communication
  • Why inclusive language matters
  • Tips for inclusive communication
  • Alternate communication methods
  • Inclusive conversation checklist
Module 3
Inclusive customer service
  • Understanding different access requirements
  • Tips for communicating with customers with different disability types (e.g. deaf or hard of hearing, blind or low vision, neurodiversity, physical disability and low mobility)
